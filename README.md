# TouchGreen
![icon](https://github.com/pal404error/TouchGreen/assets/82200759/11e92c46-9257-4d7a-b97a-d0608173f55b)

TouchGreen is a unique and innovative app designed to help you stay connected with nature throughout your busy day. With TouchGreen, you'll receive timely reminders and tips to take a moment and connect with the calming and rejuvenating power of grass. Whether you're working in an office, studying at home, or simply caught up in the hustle and bustle of life, TouchGreen gently nudges you to step outside, touch the grass, and recharge your mind and body. Embrace the beauty of nature and experience the soothing benefits of grounding yourself in the natural world with TouchGreen.
